Q: What is Pinless Dialing?
Pinless dialing allows you to apply our service to any phone you would like. When you make a call from a phone that has been registered, it is automatically used for your international calls when calling anyone of ourDirect Gold access numbers. PINLESS is a feature that recognizes the phone number that you are calling from and allows you to place a call without entering a PIN. Airtel Direct Gold accounts can recognize up to 10 different phone lines. Just dial the Airtel Direct Gold access numbers from any of your registered phones and start talking. You can register your phones by logging into your account and clicking the on "Phone Info" tab on the left side menu. We recommend that customers DO NOT register phones in dormitories, hotels, offices, hospital rooms, or any other phones that are regularly shared with many other people. When you call one of Airtel’sDirect Gold access numbers from a registered phone you will automatically be directed to dial the destination number you want to reach.

Q: I just registered... now what do I do?
Once your credit card is accepted, you can begin using Airtel Direct Gold immediately! Just dial the access number and follow the instructions. To make a call: For domestic calls: When prompted, enter the area code and the destination number followed by the # key. You do not need to enter "1" before the number. For international calls: When prompted, enter 011 + the country code + the destination number, followed by the # key.

Q: I am having trouble registering, what do I do?
Please make sure you are using a valid US bank issued MasterCard, Visa or American Express. Also, please make sure that the billing address of the credit card matches the address registered in your Airtel Direct Account. Failure to exactly match this information will result in a declined transaction. Please contact your bank to make sure there is no issue with your card. Make sure the telephone numbers you are registering are not currently registered to another user's account. Airtel Direct Gold does not allow the same number to be registered in different accounts simultaneously. If all else fails call your credit card company first to see if there are any unresolved issues with them before you contact Airtel Direct Gold customer support (1-800-460-2855).
Q: I am having trouble recharging, what do I do?
See "I am having trouble registering, what do I do?" above. Also, check to see if your billing address has changed, if you are over your credit limit, or if your card has expired. If all else fails call your credit card company first to see if there are any unresolved issues with them before you contact Airtel Direct Gold customer support (1-800-460-2855).
Q: I am having trouble logging in, what do I do?

Q: Why do I get an error when I try to open an account?
There are a variety of filters built into our sign up process to prevent fraud. You can only open one account per telephone number. If you are trying to open a second account for the same phone number you will not be allowed to. Please make sure your phone number is not registered in anyone else's account. Also, please make sure that the billing address you put into the Airtel Direct sign-up form matches the billing address of the credit card you used. Failure to exactly match this information will result in a declined transaction. Please be sure that your expiration date is correct, and that the CSV code, which is found on the back of your card (3 digits for Visa / MasterCard) (American Express is the 4 digit code on the front), is correct.
Q: I just registered. Should I receive any information from you?
You should receive a confirmation email message from Airtel Direct Gold shortly after registering. If you did not receive an email, several reasons could explain it: You typed your email incorrectly when you signed up - please log in to the website with your account number and PIN and check your member profile. Your email program on your computer has blocked our email, suspecting it to be "Spam". Please check your "junk" or "spam" email folder to see if this email was filtered. If so, please list our email address as being acceptable, so that we can continue to communicate with you. For some reason, if your account was not registered, it could be due to issues relating to your credit card. Contact customer service for further information. If you have not received an email, try logging into your account on the website using your email address and your password. If you're successful logging in, then you can rest assure that your account has already been registered.
