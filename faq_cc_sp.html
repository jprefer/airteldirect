Q: What is Airtel Direct?
Airtel Direct is a flexible prepaid calling card that enables you to make long distance calls from your cell phone, home, or office phone at significantly lower rates. Airtel Direct has no hidden charges, no connection fees and no monthly cost.
Q: How does Airtel Direct work?
Just purchase an Airtel Direct Calling Card (Click Here to see them) from any of our retail partners (Click here to see them). Call the access number on the back of the card (or click here and then on the product you bought) and you are ready to make long distance calls.
Q: What is Pinless Dialing?
Pinless dialing allows you to apply our service to any phone you would like. When you make a call from a phone that has been registered, it is automatically used for your international calls when calling anyone of our Direct access numbers. PINLESS is a feature that recognizes the phone number that you are calling from and allows you to place a call without entering a PIN. Airtel Direct accounts can recognize up to 10 different phone lines. Just dial the Airtel Direct access numbers from any of your registered phones and start talking. You can register your phones by pressing 1 to register from any phone and entering your pin number. We recommend that customers DO NOT register phones in dormitories, hotels, offices, hospital rooms, or any other phones that are regularly shared with many other people. When you call one of Airtel’s Direct access numbers from a registered phone you will automatically be directed to dial the destination number you want to reach.

Q: Are there any connection fees with Airtel Direct? Where is the small print?
No. There are no connection fees, no monthly charges and no contracts. There is a mandated payphone surcharge of 99¢ per call for calls made from payphones. Calls made through the toll free access number are charged a rate per minute of 3¢ more than the advertised rate.
